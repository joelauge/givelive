# Free Market Research Template

**Category:** Surveys  
**Website:** https://givelive.app/blog/market-research

Product feedback survey. Get started in minutes with this professionally designed, mobile-optimized GiveLive QR flow template.

## Overview

The **Market Research** template is pre-configured with an optimal flow to maximize conversions—whether you're looking to product feedback survey or engage scanners at your location.

## What's included

- Pre-built mobile-optimized pages
- QR / link Start trigger
- Integrated payment processing (where applicable)
- Automated SMS/email follow-up steps
- Real-time analytics dashboard

## How to use

1. Open https://givelive.app/blog/market-research
2. Click **Use this free template** and sign in
3. Customize copy, images, and journey steps in the builder
4. Publish and distribute your QR code
