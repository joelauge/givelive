The **Open House Sign-in** template is pre-configured with an optimal flow to maximize conversions—whether you're looking to digital register for open house visitors or engage scanners at your location.

- Pre-built mobile-optimized pages
- QR / link Start trigger

## How to use

1. Open https://givelive.app/blog/open-house
2. Click **Use this free template** and sign in
3. Customize copy, images, and journey steps in the builder
4. Publish and distribute your QR code
