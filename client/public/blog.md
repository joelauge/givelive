# GiveLive Free QR Flow Templates

Browse professionally designed templates for fundraising, real locations, churches, lead capture, webinars, concerts, surveys, tickets, raffles, quiz games, and real estate.

**Website:** https://givelive.app/blog

## How templates work

Each template is a pre-configured GiveLive journey: Start trigger (QR / link), mobile pages, optional payments, and follow-up messages. Customize copy, images, and connections in the visual builder—no code required.

## Categories

- Fundraising
- Real Locations
- Churches
- Lead Capture
- Online Webinar
- Concerts
- Surveys
- Tickets
- Raffles
- Quiz Games
- Real Estate

## Using a template

1. Open a template page below or browse at https://givelive.app/blog
2. Click **Use this free template** (sign in required to create a project).
3. Customize in the journey builder and publish.

## Template index

See individual template pages at `https://givelive.app/blog/{template-id}.md` — listed in https://givelive.app/llms.txt under **Templates**.
