# GiveLive — Intelligent QR Flows for Acquisition

GiveLive is the operating system for high-impact acquisition. Create immersive digital-to-physical journeys, capture leads instantly, and automate your follow-up workflows.

**Website:** https://givelive.app/

## Overview

GiveLive helps marketers, nonprofits, venues, churches, real-estate teams, and local businesses turn real-world touchpoints—signage, packaging, events, and in-store displays—into automated lead capture and nurture using QR-powered journeys.

## Key capabilities

- **Intelligent Journey Builder** — Visual drag-and-drop canvas for pages, SMS, email, delays, payments, and integrations.
- **Digital-to-physical bridge** — Dynamic QR codes and unique flow URLs that trigger automated workflows on scan.
- **Lead capture** — Mobile-optimized forms for name, email, phone, and custom fields.
- **Payments** — Stripe-powered donation and purchase flows.
- **Follow-up automation** — Scheduled SMS and email sequences after capture.
- **Integrations** — Follow Up Boss, HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, Constant Contact, Brevo, Zapier, Make, n8n.
- **Analytics** — Track scans, sends, conversions, and revenue per flow.

## Why physical touchpoints matter

On-site and real-location interactions build trust and produce high-intent leads. GiveLive triggers instant follow-up the moment someone scans—when response rates are highest.

## Getting started

1. Sign in at https://givelive.app/admin (free tier available).
2. Create a flow from scratch or pick a template at https://givelive.app/blog
3. Configure the Start node (QR / link), build your journey, and publish.
4. Distribute your QR code or share the flow URL.
