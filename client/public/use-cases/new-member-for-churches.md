# QR New Visitor Welcome Flows for Churches

**Audience:** Churches & Ministries  
**Template:** https://givelive.app/blog/new-member  
**Website:** https://givelive.app/use-cases/new-member-for-churches

> Never lose track of a first-time visitor again

Replace paper connection cards with a QR welcome flow: capture visitor info, send an instant welcome text, and route people to next steps automatically.

## How to launch

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/new-member-for-churches
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/new-member
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
