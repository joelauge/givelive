# QR Google Review Flows for Restaurants & Retail

**Audience:** Restaurants & Retail  
**Template:** https://givelive.app/blog/google-review  
**Website:** https://givelive.app/use-cases/google-review-for-restaurants-retail

> More 5-star reviews, fewer public complaints

Get more Google reviews from happy customers with a QR review flow on receipts and table tents — and intercept complaints before they go public.

## How to launch

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/google-review-for-restaurants-retail
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/google-review
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
