Collect beneficiary and volunteer stories with a QR video testimonial flow — authentic content for your appeals, grants, and social channels.

## How to launch

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/share-testimonial-for-nonprofits
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/share-testimonial
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
