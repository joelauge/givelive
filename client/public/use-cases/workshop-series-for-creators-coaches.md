# QR Workshop Series Signup Flows for Educators

**Audience:** Creators, Coaches & Educators  
**Template:** https://givelive.app/blog/workshop-series  
**Website:** https://givelive.app/use-cases/workshop-series-for-creators-coaches

> One signup, a whole series of engagement

Enroll students in multi-session workshops with one QR signup — series scheduling, session reminders, and completion follow-ups automated.

## How to launch

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/workshop-series-for-creators-coaches
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/workshop-series
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
