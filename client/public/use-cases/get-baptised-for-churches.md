# QR Baptism Signup Flows for Churches

**Audience:** Churches & Ministries  
**Template:** https://givelive.app/blog/get-baptised  
**Website:** https://givelive.app/use-cases/get-baptised-for-churches

> Capture the decision while hearts are stirred

Make responding to a baptism call effortless: a QR signup flow that captures decisions in the moment and routes them to your pastoral team.

## How to launch

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/get-baptised-for-churches
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/get-baptised
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
