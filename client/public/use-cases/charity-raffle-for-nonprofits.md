Run a compliant, friction-free charity raffle with QR entry, integrated donations, and automatic winner notification — no paper tickets.

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/charity-raffle-for-nonprofits
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/charity-raffle
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
