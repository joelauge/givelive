# QR Event Check-In Flows for Organizers

**Audience:** Event Organizers & Conferences  
**Template:** https://givelive.app/blog/event-checkin  
**Website:** https://givelive.app/use-cases/event-checkin-for-event-organizers

> Check-in lines are a choice. Choose not to have them.

Replace registration lines with QR self check-in — attendees check in on their phone and your post-event follow-up runs automatically.

## How to launch

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/event-checkin-for-event-organizers
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/event-checkin
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
