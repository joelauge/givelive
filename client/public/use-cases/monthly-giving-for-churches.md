# Recurring Giving Signup Flows for Churches

**Audience:** Churches & Ministries  
**Template:** https://givelive.app/blog/monthly-giving  
**Website:** https://givelive.app/use-cases/monthly-giving-for-churches

> Turn one-time givers into faithful monthly partners

Grow predictable giving with a QR recurring-donation flow — members set up monthly gifts once, on their phone, in under two minutes.

## How to launch

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/monthly-giving-for-churches
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/monthly-giving
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
