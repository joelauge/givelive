# QR Small Group Signup Flows for Churches

**Audience:** Churches & Ministries  
**Template:** https://givelive.app/blog/small-groups  
**Website:** https://givelive.app/use-cases/small-groups-for-churches

> From "interested" to "in a group" in one scan

Fill your small groups with a QR signup flow — members browse groups, register on their phone, and leaders get rosters automatically.

## How to launch

1. Open https://givelive.app/use-cases/small-groups-for-churches
2. Start from the free template at https://givelive.app/blog/small-groups
3. Customize copy, branding, and journey steps in the visual builder
4. Publish and place your QR code where your audience already is
